How Do I…

▸

…track my time?

Time tracking runs automatically once enabled in . Open the popup to see today's totals or visit for charts and your activity heatmap.

…block a site completely?

Go to , type the domain, and add it as a Blocked rule. The extension will redirect you to a block page whenever you visit that domain.

…set a daily time limit for a site?

Add the domain as a Restricted rule in . Set a time limit in minutes. Once exceeded for the day, the site will be blocked until the next day.

…change video playback speed?

You can change the playback speed using one of the following ways:

  • Use the keyboard shortcuts you've set up in the video controls tab. (Default: + / -, and .)
  • Click on the extension icon to bring up the popup and use the + / -, and . buttons there.
  • Change the speed from the video controls tab directly.

…customize keyboard shortcuts?

Configure shortcuts in . The defaults are + to increase speed, - to decrease, and . to reset. You can also set Alt+T to toggle tracking and Alt+B to block the current site from the extension's shortcut settings in chrome://extensions/shortcuts.

…change the theme or accent color?

Under , find the Appearance section. Pick light, dark, or auto theme and choose an accent color that fits your setup.

…export or back up my data?

Go to and use the Export Data button to save your usage and settings as a JSON file. Import that file later to restore your data on another profile or after a reinstall.

…whitelist a domain from tracking?

Add the domain to the whitelist in . Time spent on whitelisted sites will not be recorded.

…pause tracking temporarily?

Toggle Pause Tracking in . No data will be collected while paused.

…delete data for a specific site?

Use the Search domain to delete data field in to find and remove records for individual domains.

Minutiae

▸

Popup quick actions

Open the popup to see today's time and top sites. Alt+T toggles tracking on/off from any page. Alt+B blocks the current site immediately.

Click any heatmap day

Click a day cell to see a breakdown of which sites you visited and for how long. Click again or press "Top Sites Today" to reset.

Custom accent color

In General Settings, click the + in the color picker to add any hex color.

Watch your storage

The Privacy tab has a storage meter and auto-purge settings to keep old data from piling up.

Getting Help

Report Issues & Suggest Features

Bug reports and feature requests go to the GitHub Issues page.

When reporting a bug, include: the site URL, steps to reproduce, expected vs actual behavior, and your browser version. Screenshots help when the UI looks wrong. For video problems, mention whether the player is embedded in an iframe and paste any console errors you see.

For feature requests, a short description of what you want and why is enough to get things started.

Source Code

The full source is at github.com/intelligent-username/TimeDash. If you want to audit what the extension can access, start with manifest.json, then read the background scripts and content scripts.

Privacy Questions

Read the full Privacy Policy on the Privacy & Data tab. If something doesn't match what you see in the browser, open an issue with details.

About TimeDash

Version 1.1.6. Built as a vanilla JS Chrome extension with no frameworks and no external dependencies. All data stays on your device.
